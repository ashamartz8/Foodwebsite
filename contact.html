Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers regarding business operations and bulk sourcing.

What is the minimum wholesale order capacity?

We primarily trade in commercial bulk shipments. Minimum orders depend on product categories, usually starting from 1 FCL (Full Container Load).

Where do you source your premium grains from?

Our agro-investments and farming contracts span over premium zones in India, Pakistan, Southeast Asia, and South America.

What quality certifications do your processing hubs maintain?

All our mills and handling warehouses possess absolute international compliance certifications, matching ISO 22000, HACCP standards, and certified 100% Halal protocols.

Do you provide customized retail packaging options?

Yes, for large commercial hypermarket enterprise networks, we execute private contract white-labeling packaging under custom specifications inside automated safety loops.

How long does delivery typically take?

Transit times vary by destination. GCC delivery typically takes 3–7 business days; international sea freight to Europe or Asia runs 18–35 days depending on port of origin.
