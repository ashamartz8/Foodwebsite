About Global Foods Co.

Decades of trust, innovation, and sustainable agriculture — powering global food supply chains.

Our Story

From a regional grain trader to a global agro-investment powerhouse.

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

Global Foods Co. was established with a singular vision: to become the most trusted name in international food trading and agricultural investments. Starting from a network of certified grain suppliers across South Asia, we expanded our operations to span 25+ countries across 4 continents.

"Our foundation is quality. Our promise is reliability. Our mission is nourishment."

Over the years, we have invested heavily in processing infrastructure, cold-chain logistics, and sustainable farming partnerships. Today, we handle over 150,000 metric tons of premium food commodities annually, serving hypermarket chains, government food authorities, and enterprise distributors worldwide.

Mission, Vision & Values

The principles that guide every shipment, every partnership, every harvest.

To source, process, and deliver premium food commodities with unwavering quality standards — connecting global farmlands to local communities efficiently and sustainably.

Our Vision

Our Journey

Key milestones that shaped Global Foods Co. into what it is today.

2006

Company Founded

Global Foods Co. was established in Riyadh with a focus on premium rice and grain imports from South Asia.

2010

First Processing Mill

Launched our first dedicated milling and packaging facility, gaining ISO 22000 & HACCP certifications.

2014

GCC Expansion

Expanded distribution network across the Gulf Cooperation Council, partnering with major hypermarket chains.

2018

Eco Farming Initiative

Launched our sustainable agro-farming program supporting 1,200+ smallholder farmers with bio-organic inputs.

2022

Global Logistics Hub

Opened integrated sea-air-land logistics hub enabling direct delivery to 25+ countries with full cold-chain traceability.

2026

Digital Trade Platform

Launched proprietary B2B trade portal for enterprise clients, streamlining procurement, tracking, and quality assurance.
